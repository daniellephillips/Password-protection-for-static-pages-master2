AI Strategy & Competitive Analysis
Intel Optimization and Analysis Tools

January – March 2024

Situation

As the Developer Experience lead for Optimization Tools, I worked with the product management team on stretegic, roadmap and innovation planning for the future of Intel products. For this particualr research, we explored how AI could enhance Intel’s System Optimization tools to improve customer the experience and accelerate how our customers optimize their AI workloads. Our hardware customers rely on these tools to optimize AI workloads for training, inference, LLMs and generative AI. This research aimed to uncover how AI could be integrated to better support business and customer goals.

Tasks & Actions

I led this quarterly mixed-methods research initiative, collaborating across teams to ensure research was comprehensive, data-driven, and actionable:

  • Engaged key internal stakeholders (product, engineering, and research teams) to align research goals and secure investment in the process.
  • Leveraged SME expertise to understand our customers, software, and AI workloads while incorporating existing internal data sources.
  • Conducted competitor analysis to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities in AI tooling.
  • Validated internal assumptions through qualitative research, stakeholder interviews, and external benchmarking.
  • Synthesized insights using Dovetail, ensuring transparency and collaboration.
  • Mapped findings to strategy, translating them into user stories and product recommendations for AI-driven innovation.
  • Designed a scalable research framework for ongoing AI optimization initiatives.
Output and Results
  • Delivered a competitive landscape analysis, highlighting key gaps and innovation opportunities.
  • Validated and refined stakeholder assumptions, leading to stronger, data-driven decision-making.
  • Directly influenced product strategy, addressing unmet user needs with AI-driven solutions.
  • Created a scalable research approach for ongoing AI tooling and optimization.
  • Secured internal alignment on next steps, ensuring research insights were integrated into Jira issue tracking for release planning.
  • This research was a collaborative effort driven by me, directly shaping product innovation and future AI tooling strategies.

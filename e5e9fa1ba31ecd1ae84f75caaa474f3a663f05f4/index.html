VTune Modernization and Multinode Analysis
Intel Optimization and Analysis Tools - VTune

August 2023

Situation

Another partnership with Product Management in planning product innovation initiatives. This specific research initiative focused on modernizing VTune into Watch Tower Peak (WTPK), enhancing cloud-based multi-node performance profiling.

The research aimed to:
  • Understand market demand for cloud-based profiling solutions.
  • Identify key usability and workflow challenges.
  • Assess the competitive landscape to justify investment.
  • Develop actionable insights to improve user experience and adoption.

Tasks & Actions

I led a cross-functional research initiative to ensure findings were data-driven, actionable, and strategically aligned. My research and anlysis approach included:

  • Conducted a 12-session usability study (2-hour sessions each).
  • Identified key pain points: login issues, navigation inconsistencies, and workflow inefficiencies.
  • Mapped mental models of users to understand how they approach multi-node performance analysis.
  • Analyzed cloud-based profiling competitors (e.g., Datadog, Azure Monitor, Grafana).
  • Evaluated market trends, including the $500B cloud market (2023) projected to reach $1T by 2026.
  • Identified key gaps in existing solutions: lack of ease of use, multi-node support, and automation.

Output, Results, and Key Outcomes

This research directly shaped Intel’s cloud-based performance analysis strategy, driving key enhancements in usability, automation, and multi-node support.

With cloud computing becoming the backbone of modern workloads, investing in scalable, SaaS-based profiling tools is crucial to addressing growing performance analysis needs.
